Frequently Asked Questions

What are credits and how are they used?

Credits are used to generate images. Typically, one standard image generation costs one credit. Higher resolution or more complex generations might use more credits. Your credits reset at the start of each billing cycle.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. Your plan will remain active until the end of the current billing period, and you won't be charged again.

What happens if I run out of credits?

If you run out of credits, you can upgrade to a higher plan to get more credits immediately, or you can purchase a one-time credit top-up pack. Otherwise, your credits will be replenished at the start of your next billing cycle.
