Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as one generation?

Each time you click the 'Generate' button, it consumes one credit and produces one image based on your prompt.

Do my credits roll over?

Credits for the Inspire and Create plans expire after 3 and 5 months, respectively. For credits that never expire, choose our Elevate plan.

Can I upgrade my plan?

Yes, you can purchase a new plan at any time. Your new credit balance and expiry date will replace your previous plan's details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and UPI payments through our secure payment gateway, PayU.
